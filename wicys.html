Recommended Starting Points
  • TryHackMe
    Best starting point — guided, beginner-friendly, structured learning paths. Start here before HackTheBox.
  • HackTheBox Academy
    Next level after TryHackMe. More challenging, closer to real-world scenarios.
  • eJPT — eLearnSecurity Junior Penetration Tester
    Entry-level cert with real weight. Great first offensive security credential without years of experience required.
  • CompTIA Security+ / PenTest+
    Industry-standard baseline (Security+) and a strong next step toward penetration testing (PenTest+).
  • Digital First Responder Lab
    My IoMT threat intelligence lab — real healthcare cybersecurity scenarios showing what defending clinical environments actually looks like.
