About the film

Midnight in the War Room is Semperis's documentary on the defenders working to protect critical infrastructure and the systems people depend on. It premieres at Black Hat USA on August 5, 2026, featuring more than 50 defenders from across the industry — including Jen Easterly, Gen. David Petraeus, Chris Inglis, Dr. Mary Aiken, and Marcus Hutchins. Chaunda is featured for her work applying clinical emergency-response thinking to healthcare cybersecurity and connected medical device risk.

Additional recognition

  • WiCyS 2026 — virtual panelist, "Behind the Scenes of Midnight in the War Room."
  • Halcyon podcast — Security Gets Serious and Last Month in Security, "Healthcare Security from the Frontlines."
  • Women in Security & Privacy (WISP) — three-time DEF CON Lead Volunteer.
  • DEF CON Biohacking Village — three-time volunteer, where medical devices and AI security meet.

Why it matters

The film is a spotlight, not the mission. The work underneath it is the same before and after the premiere: protecting patients, the connected devices they depend on, and the clinicians who treat them. If that's the kind of thinking your organization needs, that's the conversation worth having.
